Welcome to the Sustainability Database

    The Sustainability Database is a public, open-source platform dedicated to cataloguing sustainable businesses across Australia and New Zealand. It serves as an educational resource aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education) by making sustainability practices transparent, accessible, and usable for consumers, researchers, and students alike.

    Our focus is on sharing knowledge, promoting ethical enterprise, and creating informed networks that support responsible decision-making in the economy.

    About Us

    We categorise businesses based on broader umbrella sectors, such as:

    • Consumer & Lifestyle: Fashion, Health & Wellness, Food & Beverage, Home & Furniture
    • Infrastructure & Environment: Construction & Real Estate, Energy, Agriculture
    • Mobility & Travel: Transport, Travel & Tourism, Automotive
    • Finance & Governance: Finance & Banking, Government
    • Education & Culture: Education, Entertainment, Events & Conferences
    • Other: Pets, Sport

    All data is maintained collaboratively through GitHub. Submissions are peer-reviewed, and all business practices listed are checked for credibility and transparency. The project is fully open-source and intended to foster collective educational benefit.
