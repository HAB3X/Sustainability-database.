Welcome to the Sustainability Database

The Sustainability Database is an open-source platform for exploring businesses in Australia and New Zealand that prioritise environmental responsibility and social ethics. This site lists sustainable companies by sector, assesses their practices, and flags potential greenwashing using transparent, peer-reviewed criteria.

Built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the project is hosted freely on GitHub Pages and maintained by a community of contributors. All data is publicly accessible and openly licensed.
